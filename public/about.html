Font Awesome Rock Paper Scissors

This is a Rock Paper Scissors game implemented with Font Awesome icons for all of the graphics.

How to Play

Click on one of the hand icons at the bottom of the screen to select your throw. The hand on the left will make the throw you choose. You will see , , or in the top middle of the screen indicating that you won, lost, or tied. In the top corners, symbols will appear representing your total number of wins; for 1, for 5, and for 25
