Rules of Rock Paper Scissors

In Rock Paper Scissors, players simultaneously "throw" one of the titular items by making the hand gesture for it: for rock, for paper, and for scissors In real life, you do this by chanting "rock! paper! scissors! shoot!", making the gesture on "shoot!". Other chants are sometimes used.
